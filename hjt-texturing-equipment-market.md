<p><p>HJT texturing equipment is essential for enhancing the efficiency of heterojunction solar cells. The market is divided into two main types: acid texturing equipment and alkaline texturing equipment. Acid texturing uses acidic solutions to create a textured wafer surface, improving light absorption. In contrast, alkaline texturing employs alkaline solutions for a different texturing approach, promoting uniformity and reducing defects. Both methods play a crucial role in optimizing solar cell performance and enhancing overall energy conversion efficiency.</p></p>
