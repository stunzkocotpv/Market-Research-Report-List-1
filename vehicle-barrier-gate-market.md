<p><strong>The Vehicle Barrier Gate Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Straight</li><li>Crank</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle barrier gate market encompasses two primary types: straight and crank barrier gates. Straight barrier gates are typically characterized by a linear, horizontal arm that swings up and down for vehicle access control, providing a straightforward solution for managing traffic flow. In contrast, crank barrier gates feature a pivoting arm that operates at an angle, allowing for a more flexible installation in constrained spaces. Both types are essential for enhancing security and regulating vehicular movement in various settings.</p></p>
