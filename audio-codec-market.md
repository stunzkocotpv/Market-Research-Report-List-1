<p><p>The audio codec market is categorized into three main types: mono, stereo, and multi-channel codecs. Mono codecs support a single audio channel, suitable for voice recordings and basic communications. Stereo codecs offer two channels, enhancing sound quality and spatial effects, ideal for music and films. Multi-channel codecs provide multiple audio channels, often utilized in surround sound systems for immersive experiences in gaming and home theater setups. Each type serves distinct user needs, driving innovations in audio reproduction technology.</p></p>
