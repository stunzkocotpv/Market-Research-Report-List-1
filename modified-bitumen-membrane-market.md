<p><p>The Modified Bitumen Membrane market includes two main types: Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene (SBS) and Atactic Polypropylene (APP). SBS membranes are known for their elasticity and flexibility, making them suitable for areas with temperature fluctuations. They are often used in cold climates. In contrast, APP membranes offer enhanced UV resistance and a more rigid structure, making them ideal for warmer climates. Both types provide effective waterproofing solutions for roofing applications, enhancing durability and performance.</p></p>
