<p><p>Acrylic resins for coatings are primarily categorized into water-based and solvent-based markets. Water-based acrylics utilize water as the primary solvent, offering low volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions and enhanced environmental safety, making them popular in residential and commercial applications. In contrast, solvent-based acrylics use organic solvents, providing excellent adhesion, durability, and weather resistance, making them ideal for industrial and outdoor environments. Each type caters to specific performance requirements, cost considerations, and environmental regulations.</p></p>
