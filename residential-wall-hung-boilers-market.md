<p><strong>The Residential Wall Hung Boilers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Regular Wall-Hung Boilers</li><li>Condens Wall-hung Boilers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The residential wall hung boilers market is primarily divided into two types: regular wall-hung boilers and condensing wall-hung boilers. Regular wall-hung boilers provide heating by burning fuel, typically maintaining a fixed efficiency. In contrast, condensing wall-hung boilers utilize advanced technology to recover additional heat from exhaust gases, offering higher efficiency and lower emissions. These variations cater to different consumer needs, with regular models often being more straightforward and cost-effective, while condensing models promote energy savings and environmental sustainability.</p></p>
