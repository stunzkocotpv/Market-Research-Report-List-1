<p><strong>The Borehole Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-stage</li><li>Multistage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The borehole pumps market comprises primarily two types: single-stage and multistage pumps. Single-stage borehole pumps are designed for applications where water is drawn from shallow depths, providing high flow rates at lower pressure. In contrast, multistage borehole pumps are engineered for deeper applications, delivering higher pressure and making them suitable for irrigation, industrial, and municipal use. The choice between these two types depends on the specific depth requirements and pressure needs of the water extraction project.</p></p>
