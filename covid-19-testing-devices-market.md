<p><p>The COVID-19 testing devices market encompasses three main types: nucleic acid testing devices, antigen testing devices, and serological testing devices. Nucleic acid testing devices, such as PCR tests, detect the virus's genetic material, providing high sensitivity and specificity. Antigen testing devices identify viral proteins, offering quicker results but with lower sensitivity. Serological testing devices measure antibodies in the blood, indicating past infection and immune response. Each type serves distinct purposes in diagnosing and monitoring COVID-19 spread and immunity levels.</p></p>
