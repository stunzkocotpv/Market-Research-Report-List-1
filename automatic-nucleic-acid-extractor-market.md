<p><ul><li>Impeticbead type</li><li>Straw type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic nucleic acid extractor market features two primary types: impetic bead type and straw type. Impetic bead extractors utilize magnetic beads that bind to nucleic acids, facilitating their separation and purification during automation. In contrast, straw-type extractors employ a specialized straw-like mechanism that draws samples through various reagents, allowing for effective nucleic acid extraction. Both types offer efficiency and reliability, catering to different laboratory requirements in genomic research, clinical diagnostics, and other molecular biology applications.</p></p>
