<p><p>The PCB relay market is segmented into four main types based on power handling capabilities. Micro power relays are designed for minimal load applications, typically under 1 watt, suitable for compact devices. Low power relays handle load requirements up to a few watts, often used in consumer electronics. Medium power relays cater to applications up to tens of watts, ideal for machinery and industrial controls. High power relays manage larger loads, exceeding tens of watts, commonly employed in heavy-duty equipment and automation systems.</p></p>
