<p><p>The automatic titrator market is segmented primarily into potential titration, coulombic titration, and other methods. Potential titration measures changes in voltage to determine the endpoint of a reaction, making it suitable for various applications in laboratories. Coulomb titration involves measuring the amount of charge passed during a reaction, providing high accuracy for specific ions. Other market types may encompass variations such as redox or complexometric titrations, catering to diverse analytical needs across pharmaceuticals, food, and environmental sectors.</p></p>
