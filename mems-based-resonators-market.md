<p><p>MEMS-based resonators are categorized into two main types: series resonance and parallel resonance. Series resonance types exhibit high quality factors and are suitable for low-frequency applications, making them ideal for sensors and filters. In contrast, parallel resonance types operate efficiently at higher frequencies, often utilized in oscillators and frequency references. Both types leverage microelectromechanical systems technology, enabling miniaturization, low power consumption, and enhanced performance in various applications, including telecommunications, consumer electronics, and automotive industries.</p></p>
