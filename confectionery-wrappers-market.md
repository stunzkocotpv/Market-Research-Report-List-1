<p><ul><li>Cast polypropylene (CPP)</li><li>Polyethylene terephthalate (PET)</li><li>Biaxially oriented polypropylene (BOPP)</li><li>Polyethylene (PE)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The confectionery wrappers market comprises various types of films, each offering unique properties. Cast polypropylene (CPP) is known for its clarity and flexibility, making it ideal for packaging sweets. Polyethylene terephthalate (PET) provides excellent barrier properties and is often used for high-end confectionery. Biaxially oriented polypropylene (BOPP) is valued for its strength and printability, commonly used in candy wrappers. Polyethylene (PE) is flexible and moisture-resistant, suitable for a range of confectionery products, including chocolates and gummies.</p></p>
