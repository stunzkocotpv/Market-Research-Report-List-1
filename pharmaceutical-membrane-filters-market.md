<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Pharmaceutical Membrane Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Microfiltration</li><li>Ultrafiltration</li><li>Nanofiltration</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmaceutical membrane filters market is categorized into several types based on pore size and separation mechanisms. Microfiltration is used for removing larger particles and bacteria, typically with pore sizes of 0.1 to 10 microns. Ultrafiltration operates at smaller pore sizes (1-100 nanometers), effectively separating macromolecules and smaller solutes. Nanofiltration lies between these two, targeting divalent ions and small organic molecules, while "Others" encompasses specialized filtration methods such as reverse osmosis and hybrid systems for unique applications in pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
