<p><ul><li>Tube</li><li>Fin</li><li>Plate</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The engine oil cooling device market consists of three main types: tube, fin, and plate coolers. Tube coolers utilize a series of tubes to circulate oil, promoting efficient heat transfer. Fin coolers increase surface area and enhance cooling through external fins that dissipate heat. Plate coolers consist of stacked plates that create multiple paths for oil flow, optimizing cooling efficiency. Each type is designed to improve engine performance and longevity by regulating oil temperature under varying operating conditions.</p></p>
