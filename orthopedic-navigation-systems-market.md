<p><strong>The Orthopedic Navigation Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CT-Free Navigation Systems</li><li>Image-Free Navigation Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Orthopedic navigation systems enhance surgical precision by providing real-time guidance. CT-Free Navigation Systems utilize pre-operative imaging data without relying on computed tomography, allowing for less radiation exposure and quicker setups. Image-Free Navigation Systems, on the other hand, do not depend on any pre-existing images, instead using real-time tracking of surgical instruments relative to the patient's anatomy. Both systems aim to improve surgical outcomes, reduce complications, and streamline orthopedic procedures by enhancing accuracy during interventions.</p></p>
