<p><p>The diving snorkel market is segmented into three main types: wet, semi-dry, and dry. Wet snorkels allow water to enter, providing a natural breathing experience but requiring skill to manage water. Semi-dry snorkels feature a splash guard that reduces water ingress, making them suitable for casual snorkelers. All dry snorkels are designed to prevent water from entering completely, offering a more comfortable experience for longer underwater excursions. Each type caters to different user preferences and skill levels.</p></p>
