<p><ul><li>All-electric Type Retarders</li><li>Electromagnetic Type Retarders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric retarders market comprises two primary types: all-electric type retarders and electromagnetic type retarders. All-electric type retarders utilize electric motors to provide braking force, offering precise control and efficiency, often employed in electric vehicles and hybrid systems. In contrast, electromagnetic type retarders use electromagnetic forces to generate resistance against vehicle motion, enhancing braking performance without relying solely on traditional friction brakes. Both technologies contribute to improved vehicle safety, reduced wear, and energy efficiency in heavy-duty applications.</p></p>
