<p><p>The education apps market can be categorized into cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based education apps are hosted on remote servers, allowing access from any device with internet connectivity. This flexibility promotes collaboration, scalability, and lower upfront costs, making it suitable for institutions of all sizes. Conversely, on-premise education apps are installed directly on local servers, offering greater control over data security and customization but often requiring higher initial investments and ongoing maintenance. Each type serves different institutional needs.</p></p>
