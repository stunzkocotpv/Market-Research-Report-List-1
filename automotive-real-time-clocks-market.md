<p><p>The automotive real-time clocks (RTCs) market includes various types catering to specific requirements. Integrated quartz crystal RTCs enhance accuracy and stability, embedded directly into devices. Ultra-low-power RTCs are designed for energy efficiency, operating with minimal power consumption, crucial for battery-operated systems in vehicles, ensuring accurate timekeeping without draining resources.</p></p>
