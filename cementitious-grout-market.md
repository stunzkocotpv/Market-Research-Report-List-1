<p><p>Cementitious grout is categorized mainly into two types: General Purpose and High Performance. General Purpose grout is ideal for standard applications, offering adequate strength and workability for tasks like filling gaps and stabilizing structures. In contrast, High Performance grout is engineered for demanding conditions, featuring superior adhesion, enhanced durability, and resistance to environmental factors. This type is often used in critical applications such as precast concrete elements and structural repairs, ensuring longevity and reliability in challenging environments.</p></p>
