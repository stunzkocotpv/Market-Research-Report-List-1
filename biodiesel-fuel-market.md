<p><strong>The Biodiesel Fuel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pure Biodiesel Fuel</li><li>Non-pure Biodiesel Fuel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biodiesel fuel market consists of two main types: pure biodiesel fuel and non-pure biodiesel fuel. Pure biodiesel, often referred to as B100, is derived entirely from renewable sources such as vegetable oils or animal fats, ensuring a lower carbon footprint. Non-pure biodiesel, typically blended with conventional diesel, includes various mixtures like B20 or B5, which contain 20% or 5% biodiesel, respectively. This blend enhances diesel's environmental performance while maintaining engine compatibility and performance.</p></p>
