<p><p>Thermometer guns are categorized into two main types: non-contact and contact types. Non-contact thermometers measure temperature from a distance, using infrared technology to provide quick readings without physical contact, making them ideal for screening in healthcare and public spaces. In contrast, contact thermometers require direct contact with the object or person, providing more accurate readings but taking longer to measure. Both types are essential in various industries, including healthcare, food safety, and manufacturing, catering to specific needs for temperature assessment.</p></p>
