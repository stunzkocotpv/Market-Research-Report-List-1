<p><p>The Optical Inspection Equipment market is segmented into two main types: Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) Equipment and Manual Optical Inspection Equipment. AOI Equipment utilizes advanced imaging technology and software algorithms to automatically detect defects in components and assemblies during the manufacturing process, enhancing efficiency and accuracy. Conversely, Manual Optical Inspection Equipment relies on human operators to visually assess products for quality control, allowing for nuanced inspections but at a slower pace. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring product quality in various industries.</p></p>
