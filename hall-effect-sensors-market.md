<p><strong>The Hall-Effect Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Threshold Type</li><li>Linear Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hall-Effect sensors are classified mainly into two types: threshold and linear. Threshold type sensors provide a binary output, detecting magnetic fields above or below a specified level, making them ideal for on/off applications such as proximity sensing and magnetic switching. In contrast, linear type sensors offer an output that varies proportionally with the strength of the magnetic field, allowing for precise measurements in applications such as position sensing and current sensing. Both types cater to diverse industrial and automotive sectors.</p></p>
