<p><p>Power inductors in automotive applications are categorized by inductance values, primarily focusing on two segments: 0.33 - 4.7 μH and above 4.7 μH. The 0.33 - 4.7 μH range is typically used in applications requiring higher current handling and rapid switching, often found in power converters and motor drives. In contrast, inductors above 4.7 μH are suited for energy storage and filtering in power supply systems, essential for efficient operation in advanced automotive electronics, including battery management and electric vehicle systems.</p></p>
