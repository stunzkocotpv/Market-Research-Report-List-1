<p><p>The automotive silicone adhesives and sealants market consists primarily of two types: single component and double component. Single component adhesives are ready-to-use, requiring no mixing, and typically cure through moisture in the air, making them convenient for various applications. Double component adhesives, on the other hand, require the combination of two parts before application, which allows for enhanced performance characteristics, such as improved adhesion or heat resistance. Both types are essential for ensuring durability and effectiveness in automotive manufacturing and repairs.</p></p>
