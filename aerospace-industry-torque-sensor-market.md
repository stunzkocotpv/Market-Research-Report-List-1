<p><p>The aerospace industry torque sensor market comprises two primary types: rotary torque sensors and non-contacting torque sensors. Rotary torque sensors measure torque in rotating systems, providing precise feedback for engine performance and component testing. Non-contacting torque sensors utilize electromagnetic or optical principles to gauge torque without physical interference, enhancing durability and allowing for real-time measurements. Both sensor types are crucial for optimizing aircraft performance, maintaining safety standards, and improving the efficiency of aerospace systems.</p></p>
