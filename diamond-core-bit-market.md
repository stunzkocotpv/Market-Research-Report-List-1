<p><p>The diamond core bit market comprises several types tailored for specific drilling applications. **PDC drill bits** utilize polycrystalline diamond compact technology for efficient rock cutting. **TSP drill bits** incorporate thermally stable polycrystalline diamonds, enhancing durability for hard formations. **Impregnated drill bits** feature diamond particles embedded in a matrix, ideal for deep drilling into challenging substrates. **Core bits** are designed to extract cylindrical samples from the earth. **Others** encompass various specialized bits catering to niche applications within the drilling industry.</p></p>
