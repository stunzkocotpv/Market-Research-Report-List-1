<p><strong>The ITO Transparent Conductive Films Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Resistance Film</li><li>High Resistance Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ITO (Indium Tin Oxide) transparent conductive films are categorized into two main types based on resistance levels: low resistance and high resistance films. Low resistance films typically exhibit lower electrical resistivity, making them ideal for applications requiring high conductivity, such as in touchscreens, LCDs, and solar cells. In contrast, high resistance films are used where slightly higher resistivity is acceptable, often in sensor applications or in situations where enhanced optical transparency is prioritized. These distinctions cater to diverse technological needs.</p></p>
