<p><strong>The Protein Assays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dye-Binding Assays</li><li>Copper-Ion-Based Assays</li><li>Test Strip-Based Assays</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The protein assays market includes various types, each leveraging unique principles for protein quantification. Dye-binding assays utilize colorimetric changes from dye-protein interactions for measurement. Copper-ion-based assays, such as the Biuret method, rely on copper-binding proteins leading to color development. Test strip-based assays offer rapid, user-friendly quantification using immobilized reagents on strips. Other methods encompass techniques like enzyme-linked immunosorbent assays (ELISA) and mass spectrometry, catering to diverse research and clinical needs across biotechnology and pharmaceutical sectors.</p></p>
