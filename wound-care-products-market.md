<p><p>The wound care products market includes various types designed to promote healing and prevent infection. Collagen products enhance tissue regeneration. Antimicrobial dressings fight infection with embedded agents. Foam dressings provide cushioning and absorb exudate, while hydrocolloids create a gel-like environment for moist healing. Film dressings offer a protective barrier while allowing oxygen exchange. Hydrogels maintain moisture and soothe tissue. Alginate dressings, derived from seaweed, absorb fluid and promote healing in moderate to heavily exuding wounds. Each type targets specific wound care needs.</p></p>
