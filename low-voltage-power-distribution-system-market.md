Fixed type systems feature stationary components that are permanently installed, offering simplicity and reliability for basic applications. In contrast, drawer type systems incorporate modular, removable drawers that allow for easy access and maintenance of circuit breakers and other equipment, providing flexibility for upgrades and repairs. Both types cater to various industrial, commercial, and residential needs, ensuring efficient power distribution and management across multiple settings.</p></p>
