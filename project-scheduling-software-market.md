<p><p>Project scheduling software is categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. On-premises software, however, is installed locally on a company's servers, offering greater control over data security and customization but requiring significant investment in hardware and maintenance. Both types cater to different organizational needs and preferences in project management.</p></p>
