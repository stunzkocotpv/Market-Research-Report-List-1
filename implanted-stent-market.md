<p><p>The implanted stent market comprises three primary types of biomaterials: metallic, polymer, and natural biomaterials. Metallic biomaterials, such as stainless steel and cobalt-chromium, provide strength and durability for vascular support. Polymer biomaterials offer flexibility and can be engineered for controlled drug release, enhancing healing. Natural biomaterials, derived from biological sources, promote biocompatibility and reduce the risk of adverse reactions. Each type caters to specific clinical needs and patient conditions, driving innovation and improvements in stent technology.</p></p>
