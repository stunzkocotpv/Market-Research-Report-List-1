<p><strong>The PoE Connected Lighting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Powered Device Controllers and Ics</li><li>Power Sourcing Equipment Controllers and Ics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PoE connected lighting market includes two key types: Powered Device (PD) Controllers and Integrated Circuits (ICs), and Power Sourcing Equipment (PSE) Controllers and ICs. PD Controllers are responsible for receiving power and data over Ethernet to control lighting fixtures efficiently. In contrast, PSE Controllers manage the power delivery from the network to multiple devices, ensuring optimal energy distribution. Both types are essential for enabling smart lighting solutions that enhance energy efficiency and operational flexibility in various environments.</p></p>
